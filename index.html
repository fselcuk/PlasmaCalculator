Plasma Parameters Calculator

Plasma describes a state of matter in which the charged particles of decomposed atoms become a collective assemblage, where none of the particles are actually free, but strongly affected by the EM fields of each neighbor particle. The system of charged particles responds collectively to electromagnetic forces.
This calculator will compute the values for:

Angular Frequency ω ci = ElementaryCharge . MagneticField Ion Mass = e . B m
